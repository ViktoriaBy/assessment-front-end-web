Rockwall, my City

Rockwall is a city in Rockwall County, Texas, United States, which is part of the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex.

It is the county seat of Rockwall County. The U.S. Census Bureau estimate's Rockwall's 2019 population to be 45,888.

The name Rockwall is derived from a naturally jointed geological formation, which has the appearance of an artificial wall.
